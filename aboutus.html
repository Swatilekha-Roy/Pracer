About Us

PRACER is an ed-tech company which aims at providing commerce students online live affordable 1:1 mentoring by curated mentors who can guide them functionally and psychologically in acing their CA/CS/CMA exams.

The coaching classes provide understanding of the subjects but no one teaches us how to plan & strategise the studies such that we can retain content and reproduce well in exams.

PRACER’s mentoring program is specially designed for students to help and empower students to crack these exams for the first time with higher chances of scoring a rank.

In this program, students are matched with mentors who are qualified CA to become their friend, philosopher & guide in their journey of becoming a CA. This bespoke mentorship program providing 1:1 mentoring sessions and include the following:

  • Preparation of a customized detailed study plan
  • Rigorous follow-ups for progress & improvement strategies
  • Psychologically guiding students to stay focus & motivated
  • Scheduling test papers & its analysis
